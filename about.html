About

I am studying BS Financial Technology at FAST NUCES, Lahore, and I am building my way through the overlap of finance, software, and AI. I am interested in using modern tools, but more importantly, in understanding how they work underneath and where they can create practical value.

Finance interests me because small decisions compound quickly, and software gives those decisions structure, speed, and scale. I like working on products that make financial behavior easier to understand, whether that means clearer budgets, better analytics, or systems that turn raw data into useful decisions.

I am also a Co-Founder of FAST Fellows and have taught Python and chess to 150+ students over 3+ years. Teaching has made me comfortable breaking technical ideas down for people at different levels without making the subject feel smaller than it is.

Chess is a supporting part of how I work. As a national-level player with a 2200+ rating on Chess.com, I have learned to think a few steps ahead, stay calm under pressure, and review mistakes methodically instead of emotionally.
